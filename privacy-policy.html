Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 23, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information on Darklands Hosting.

1. What We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address, username, billing details.
  • Usage Data: IP address, login times, server logs and activities.
  • Payment Information: Processed securely by third-party gateways (e.g., PayPal, Stripe). We do not store your full payment details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your hosting account.
  • To provide customer support and service updates.
  • To process payments and manage billing.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Sharing Your Data

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share data only with:
    • Payment processors to complete transactions.
    • Law enforcement if required by applicable law.

4. Cookies & Analytics

  • We use cookies and tracking technologies to:
    • Analyze traffic and usage patterns.
    • Enhance website performance and user experience.
  • You can disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Security

  • We implement standard security measures to protect your data.
  • No system is 100% secure; you use our services at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time.
  • You may also request to close your account permanently.
  • To make a request, email us at support@darklands.online.

7. Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy periodically.
  • We will notify users via website or email of any significant changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, feel free to contact us at support@darklands.online.
